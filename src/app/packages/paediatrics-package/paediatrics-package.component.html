Pediatric Health Packages

We know that when it comes to the health of your child, there are no compromises. We are on the same page as you. At Lotus Hospitals we have designed complete health check-up packages that ensure early detection, prevention and prompt treatment of your child. We recommend undergoing regular master check-ups once a year so that your child is healthy and happy.

Well Baby Screening (Basic)

Birth to 1 year of age

This Package Includes:

• Growth & Developmental Assessment and Counselling

• Nutritionist / Dietician Consultation

• Routine Blood Tests (CBP, AST, ALT, Na/K/Ca/P/ALP, S.Creat & RBS)

• Routine Urine Analysis

• Routine Stool Analysis

Well Baby Screening (Extensive)

Birth to 1 year of age

This Package Includes:

• Growth, Developmental Assessment and Counselling by Consultant Pediatrician

• Pediatric Surgeon Consultation

• Nutritionist / Dietician Consultation

• Ultrasound of whole Abdomen

• Routine Blood tests (CBP, AST, ALT, Na/K/Ca/P/ALP, S.Creat, RBS)

• Routine Urine Analysis

• Routine Stool Analysis
