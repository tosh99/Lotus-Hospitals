Careers at Lotus Hospitals

Lotus Hospitals has a nationally recognised Newborn & Intensive Care Programme and a 3 Year Programme in Pediatrics Training – Affiliated with the National Board of Examination which is known to produce some of the best pediatricians in the country.

By choosing to work with Lotus Hospitals, you will add to your practice, not only in years but enriching experiences. We offer a good and fulfilling work environment.

Current Positions that are open:

Designation : Resident Doctors

Qualification : MBBS

Experience : 0 - 5 years

Shift : Rotational

Occupancy : Immediate

If you are interested to work with Lotus Hospitals, please send in your résumé to hr@lotushospitals.in or fill in the Career Form. You may also contact the Administration Department at Lakdi ka Pul, between 9 am and 6 pm.
