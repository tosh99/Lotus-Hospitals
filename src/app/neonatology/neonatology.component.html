Neonatology at Lotus Hospitals

We know how critical it is to take care of your newborn. So when they need a little more love and attention, you can rely on us to provide the utmost care for their early recovery. Our expert neonatal care providers ensure their healthy and speedy stabilisation so that you can go home with them as soon as possible.

We take very good care of your newborn so that they still feel the warmth of their mother while being nursed back to health.

Early Recovery & Rehabilitation of Your Newborn

Having successfully restored the health of many premature newborns, some even born with an exceptionally low body weight of 1.1 pounds, we have come to be known for our Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU). The NICU at Lotus Hospitals being one of the biggest in the world, is fully equipped with global standard healthcare monitors and all ancillary support equipment.
