For Doctors

Postgraduate Medical & Nursing Education

We offer higher specialist training for doctors aspiring to pursue a career in Pediatric Critical Care and Neonatology. Our institute is accredited for training for:

Pediatric Critical Care (Pediatric Intensive Care)

We are accredited by the I.A.P Intensive Care Chapter ( Indian Academy of Pediatrics – Intensive Care Chapter & College of Pediatric Critical Care ), for training pediatricians aspiring to become fully trained and confident ‘ Pediatric Intensivists ‘. Eligibility is an MD Pediatrics , DNB Pediatrics Degree, DCH ( Diploma in Child Health ) degree from any recognized university ( MCI – Medical Council of India Recognized Institution ). The course is a one year programme for those who have the MD Pediatrics or DNB Pediatrics degree, and a two year programme for those who have the DCH degree. Interviews are conducted on November 1st every year for two positions for training. The course commences on December 1st every year and the exit examination is conducted by the Pediatric Critical Care Council every year in mid December. The detailed information about the course can be obtained from the website of the Indian Academy of Pediatrics – Intensive Care Chapter ( www.piccindia.com ). Prospective candidates may access the College tab on this website, and review the information for the IDPCCM – One Year Fellowship Course and follow the instructions as well as requirements and eligibility for the training. All applicants are requested to write and email to: hr@lotushospitals.in with the text entered in the subject heading of the email as : Application for PICU Fellowship, and attach their curriculum vitae. The hospital will contact and communicate with the candidates.

Neonatology (Neonatal Medicine)

We are accredited by the I.A.P. Neonatology Chapter ( Indian Academy of Pediatrics – Neonatology Chapter, for training pediatricians aspiring to become fully trained and confident ‘ Neonatologists ‘. Eligibility is an MD Pediatrics , DNB Pediatrics Degree, DCH ( Diploma in Child Health ) Degree from any recognized university ( MCI – Medical Council of India Recognized Institution ). The course is a one year programme for MDPediatrics / DNB Pediatrics candidates, and a one and a half years course for DCH candidates Interviews are conducted on November 1st every year for two positions for training. The course commences on January 1st every year and the exit examination is conducted by the Indian Academy of Pediatrics – Neonatology Chapter twice a year in the month of July and January.

All applicants are requested to write and email to: hr@lotushospitals.in with the text entered in the subject heading of the email as : Application for NICU Fellowship, and attach their curriculum vitae. The hospital will contact and communicate with the candidates.

DNB Pediatrics (Diplomate of the National Board – Pediatrics Course) :

We are accredited by the National Board of Examinations, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India, New Delhi , for training pediatricians aspiring to become fully trained and confident ‘ Pediatricians ‘. Eligibility is an MBBS Degree or a , DCH ( Diploma in Child Health ) Degree from any recognized university ( MCI – Medical Council of India Recognized Institution ). The course is a three year programme for MBBS candidates and two years for DCH candidates. Information regarding eligibility, screening tests / interviews may be obtained from the website of the National Board of Examinations, New Delhi : www.natboard.edu . Selection of candidates is centralized and fully in the purview of the National Board of Examinations, New Delhi. We are accredited to train one MBBS ( Primary ) Candidate and one DCH ( Secondary ) candidate per year.

The DNB Pediatrics Programme at Lotus Hospitals for Women & Children has been in existence for a decade now. Our overall pass rate ( first attempt ) for both primary and secondary postgraduate students so far has been 90% ! Over the last four years, our pass rate has been consistently 100%. For Nurses

We offer thorough, structured training for nurses wishing to pursue a career / fulfil requirements for nursing training in Pediatrics and Neonatology. Eligibility is : GNM Degree / B.Sc Nursing Degree. Training is handson and rigorous and involves direct patient care after sufficient instruction and mentorship by senior nursing staff. The duration of the training is flexible, but we recommend nurses to train with us for two full years to gain exposure and confidence in looking after newborns and children in intensive care units, wards, and emergency department.

Interested nurses may email us their CVs to : hr@lotushospitals.in with the text in the subject header ‘ Application for Nursing Training ‘ to the Nursing Manager.
