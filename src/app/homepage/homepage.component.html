Your Health First

The legacy of Lotus Hospitals is that of Dr. V.S.V. Prasad and one that precedes Lotus Hospitals itself. Having studied medicine and completed his residency in the US and UK, Dr. Prasad returned to India to set up a paediatric care hospital of global standards. Providing quality care is paramount to us and came to realisation with the establishment of Lotus Hospitals in 2006. All our hospitals are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and technology to ensure world-class treatment for you and your little ones.
